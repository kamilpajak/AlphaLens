## What happened

### Phase A auto-pivot (PRE-COMMITTED)

The pre-committed Phase A overlay sanity gate fired before holdout look. Using BAA10Y as a credit-spread proxy on TRAIN (BAMLH0A0HYM2 publicly available only from 2023-05-02, insufficient TRAIN coverage), correlation(spread_z_t, forward 21d SPY return_t+1:t+21) = **+0.047** full sample (gate required ≤ −0.05). Decade-window split showed sign instability (+0.115 first half / −0.090 second half; absolute drift 0.205, within stability gate ≤0.4 but failing directionality gate).

This empirically confirmed FATAL #1 from perplexity adversarial review: a linear-interp gate that reduces equity exposure on widening spreads would have de-levered into recovery rallies (notably 2020 and 2017-2019 dynamics dominating the first half of TRAIN).

Per `success_criteria.auto_pivot_triggers.l4_overlay_sanity_failed`: drop Layer 4 from PRIMARY → run pure-Layer-2 long-only safe-decile under same |t|≥3.50 threshold.

### Phase B holdout (post auto-pivot)

Pure-Layer-2 long-only equal-weighted bottom-quintile Merton-PD safe-decile on SP1500 PIT (excl. top-50 mega-caps, excl. top-quintile distress always). 24-month holdout 2024-04-30 → 2026-04-30, monthly rebalance stride=21d, 5-phase audit at offsets {0,4,8,12,16}.

**5-phase αt range = 4.08** (gate ≤0.5). One of the most extreme phase-aliasing examples observed in the program. Same strategy gives −83% to +98% annualized α 4F depending on which trading day of the month rebalance falls. Mean αt = +0.24 — marginal, indistinguishable from zero.

Mid-cap regime in 2024-2026 explains the aliasing: 21-day rebalance phase falling adjacent to specific mid-cap rally weeks dramatically changes which "safe" names end up held during surges. Mid-cap 2024-2025 was volatile; bottom-quintile-PD names (high V/D ratios, low realized vol) clustered in defensive sectors that lagged the rally most months but had concentrated catch-up periods.

Strategy SMB beta = −0.69 on phase 0 (negative SMB loading = LARGE-cap tilt within the universe). Despite top-50 megacap exclusion, the bottom quintile of Merton-PD ranking systematically picks larger names within the remaining 1450 universe — these underperformed the small-cap rally that dominated several stretches of the holdout.

## Auto-pivot caveat (data substitution)

The Phase A overlay sanity check used BAA10Y as proxy for HY OAS (BAMLH0A0HYM2). Literature (Gilchrist-Zakrajsek 2012, Adrian-Crump-Moench 2013) confirms IG and HY credit spreads are highly correlated and have aligned direction-of-association with forward equity returns. Proxy substitution is well-grounded but should be flagged as a data-engineering enhancement post pre-reg lock; was deemed acceptable because:
1. Ground truth direction (negative correlation) is canonical and replicated in many studies.
2. Pre-reg auto_pivot rule is monotonic in directionality, not magnitude — substitute proxy must give the SAME PASS/FAIL conclusion.
3. The actual TRAIN result was POSITIVE (+0.047), strong qualitative signal of inversion regardless of which spread series is used.

## Key methodological lessons

1. **Pre-reg discipline held end-to-end.** Adversarial review (perplexity, 2026-05-04) flagged FATAL #1 ex-ante; pre-committed Phase A gate caught it on TRAIN; auto-pivot triggered without post-hoc reasoning. Discipline worked as designed.

2. **Phase-aliasing is real.** With 21-day stride on a 24-month holdout (only 23 observations per phase), the same strategy can produce αt from −1.81 to +2.27 depending on phase offset. Single-phase results are sample-of-one artifacts. Multi-phase audit is mandatory; phase-aliasing identified previously in tri-factor 2026-04-29 + mom+lowvol 2026-04-29 + quality+momentum 2026-04-30; this is the **most extreme example yet observed (αt range 4.08)**.

3. **Compound-hypothesis "novel structure" can collapse mid-flight.** The Layer-2 × Layer-4 architecture was supposed to add Bonferroni-friendly novelty. After Phase A auto-pivot dropped Layer 4, the experiment effectively became "another low-leverage cross-sectional test" — overlapping with burnt `price_factor_search_2026_04_29` class (4/4 FAIL). Future compound experiments should pre-validate each layer's standalone behavior before locking the joint hypothesis.

4. **HY OAS data publicly restricted.** BAMLH0A0HYM2 only goes back to 2023-05-02 in FRED public API (ICE/BofA copyright restriction). For any future credit-spread overlay experiment, must either:
   - Use BAA10Y (Moody's BAA Yield − 10Y Treasury, full coverage 1986+), OR
   - Subscribe to ICE Data Indices or premium FRED tier.
