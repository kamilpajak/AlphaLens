# Fundamental Analysis Report: Plexus Corp (PLXS)
**Date:** July 8, 2025
**Ticker:** PLXS (NASDAQ)
**Sector:** Technology
**Industry:** Electronic Components

## 1. Company Profile
Plexus Corp (PLXS) is a leading provider of Electronic Manufacturing Services (EMS). The company provides design, engineering, and manufacturing services for complex products in demanding markets, including Industrial, Medical, and Aerospace and Defense. Headquartered in Neenah, Wisconsin, Plexus operates globally, helping original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) bring products from concept to market through a "Product Realization" value proposition.

## 3. Financial Statement Analysis

### Income Statement Trends
Plexus has demonstrated consistent revenue growth over the past few years.
*   **Revenue Growth:** Revenue increased from $3.96B in fiscal 2024 to approximately $4.03B (projected/estimated for fiscal 2025). Quarterly revenue growth is healthy at **9.6% YOY**.
*   **Profitability:** Net income has improved significantly from $111.8M in 2024 to $172.9M (TTM). This indicates successful cost management or a shift toward higher-margin projects.
*   **Margins:** Gross margins remain thin (~10%), which is typical for the EMS industry. However, the operating margin at 5.07% suggests efficient SG&A management.

### Balance Sheet Strength
*   **Liquidity:** The company holds **$306.76 Million** in cash and cash equivalents. With total current assets of $2.40B against current liabilities of $1.52B, the current ratio is approximately **1.58**, indicating a solid short-term liquidity position.
*   **Inventory:** Inventory levels are high at **$1.23 Billion**. While common in the EMS sector to manage supply chain risks, this represents a significant portion of assets and a potential risk if demand softens.
*   **Debt:** Long-term debt is manageable at **$91.99 Million**, especially when compared to shareholder equity of **$1.45 Billion**. The low debt-to-equity ratio provides Plexus with significant financial flexibility for future expansions.

### Cash Flow Analysis
*   **Operating Cash Flow:** Plexus generated **$249.23 Million** in operating cash flow for the most recent annual period, showing a strong ability to convert earnings into cash.
*   **Investing Activities:** Capital expenditures were **$95.26 Million**, reflecting continued investment in manufacturing capacity and technological capabilities.
*   **Financing Activities:** The company has been active in share repurchases, with **$65.34 Million** spent on buybacks in the latest period, signaling management's confidence in the company's valuation.

## 4. Market and Valuation Insights
*   **Analyst Sentiment:** Analysts are generally bullish on PLXS, with **3 "Hold"**, **1 "Buy"**, and **2 "Strong Buy"** ratings. The average target price is **$200.80**.
*   **Technical Position:** The stock is currently trading above its 50-day moving average ($203.55) and its 200-day moving average ($160.57), indicating a strong upward momentum over the last few months.
*   **Valuation Gap:** The significant difference between the Trailing PE (36.95) and Forward PE (15.36) suggests that the market expects a major acceleration in earnings over the next twelve months.

## 5. Strategic Outlook & Risks
### Opportunities
*   **Market Diversification:** Plexus focuses on highly regulated, complex markets (Medical, A&D) which typically have stickier customer relationships and higher barriers to entry than consumer electronics.
*   **Operational Efficiency:** The improvement in net income despite relatively modest revenue growth suggests that Plexus is optimizing its global footprint and production efficiency.

### Risks
*   **Supply Chain & Inventory:** The $1.23B inventory position is a double-edged sword. While it protects against shortages, it exposes the company to write-downs if customer forecasts are not met.
*   **Concentration:** EMS providers are often dependent on a few large customers. Any loss of a major contract would significantly impact the top line.
