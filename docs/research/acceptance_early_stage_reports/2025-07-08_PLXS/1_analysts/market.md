However, the histogram has begun to contract, and the MACD line has slightly ticked down from its July 3rd peak of 1.95. The current reading of 56.21 indicates that the "froth" has been removed, and the stock is back in a neutral zone, providing room for a move in either direction without being technically overextended.

### Actionable Insights and Trading Strategy
1.  **Support Zone (Buy on Weakness):** The area between **$134.50 and $135.20** represents strong confluence support (Bollinger Middle and VWMA). Traders looking for an entry should monitor for a bounce in this zone.
