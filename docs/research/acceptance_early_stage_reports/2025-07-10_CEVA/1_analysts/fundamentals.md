### **1. Company Profile & Business Model**
CEVA, Inc. is a leading licensor of silicon and software intellectual property (IP) for the semiconductor industry. The company specializes in Digital Signal Processors (DSPs), AI engines, wireless platforms, and complementary software for sensor fusion, image enhancement, and voice processing. 

**Key Market Segments:**
*   **Wireless:** 5G, Wi-Fi 6/7, and Bluetooth IP.
*   **IoT & Sensing:** Solutions for wearable tech, smart home devices, and industrial IoT.
*   **Automotive & AI:** High-growth segments involving ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) and edge AI processing.

CEVA's business model is high-margin, generating revenue from two primary streams:
1.  **Licensing & Services:** Upfront fees for access to their technology.
2.  **Royalties:** Per-unit fees based on the volume of chips shipped by customers using CEVA IP.
