### **1. Company Profile**
Bel Fuse Inc. (BELFB) is a New Jersey-based manufacturer that designs and markets a broad array of products used in networking, telecommunications, high-speed data transmission, commercial aerospace, military, broadcasting, transportation, and consumer electronics. Its product portfolio includes magnetic solutions, power solutions and protection, and connectivity solutions. The company operates globally with a significant presence in the United States, Europe, and Asia.
