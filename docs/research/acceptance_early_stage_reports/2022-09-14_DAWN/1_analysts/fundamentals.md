### Fundamental Analysis Report: Day One Biopharmaceuticals Inc. (DAWN)
**Date:** September 14, 2022  
**Ticker:** DAWN (NASDAQ)  
**Sector/Industry:** Healthcare / Biotechnology

---

#### **1. Company Overview**
Day One Biopharmaceuticals is a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company focused on developing and commercializing targeted therapies for genetically defined cancers. Their primary focus is on pediatric and adult oncology, where there is a high unmet medical need. As of mid-2022, the company’s lead program is tovorafenib (DAY101), an oral, brain-penetrant, highly selective type II RAF kinase inhibitor.

#### **2. Financial Performance (as of Q2 2022)**
As a clinical-stage biotech, DAWN does not currently generate product revenue. Its financial health is measured by its cash position, burn rate, and clinical progression.

*   **Revenue:** $0 (Consistent with clinical-stage status).
*   **Net Loss:** For the quarter ended June 30, 2022, the company reported a net loss of **$36.53 million**, compared to **$27.75 million** in Q1 2022. This increasing loss reflects the ramp-up in clinical trial activities.
*   **Operating Expenses:** 
    *   **Research & Development (R&D):** $22.56 million (Q2 2022). This is a significant portion of their spend, dedicated to advancing tovorafenib and other pipeline candidates.
    *   **Selling, General & Administrative (SG&A):** $14.16 million (Q2 2022).

#### **3. Balance Sheet & Liquidity**
DAWN maintains a very robust and "clean" balance sheet, which is a primary strength for a biotech company in the current macro environment.

*   **Cash and Liquidity:** As of June 30, 2022, DAWN held approximately **$394.86 million** in cash and short-term investments.
*   **Debt:** The company has minimal debt, with total liabilities of only **$13.4 million**.
*   **Cash Runway:** With a quarterly cash burn (operating cash flow) of roughly **$30 million**, the company has an estimated runway of **over 12 quarters (3 years)**. This provides ample time to reach major clinical milestones and potential regulatory approval without the immediate need for dilutive financing.

#### **4. Market Performance & Sentiment**
*   **Price Action:** DAWN has seen significant momentum. The stock's 50-day moving average ($17.61) is well above its 200-day moving average ($10.50), signaling a strong bullish trend over the summer of 2022.
*   **Valuation:** Market Capitalization stands at approximately **$2.22 billion**. The Price-to-Book ratio is **5.03**, reflecting market optimism regarding the pipeline's value relative to its current assets.
*   **Analyst Outlook:** The consensus among analysts is a **"Hold"** (8 ratings), with a price target of **$21.50**. This suggests that while analysts recognize the company's potential, they may be waiting for more definitive data from the FIREFLY-1 or other pivotal trials before upgrading.

#### **5. Actionable Insights for Traders**
*   **Clinical Readout Dependency:** Traders should monitor for upcoming data readouts regarding tovorafenib. As a clinical-stage company, the stock price is highly sensitive to trial results.
*   **Strong Defensive Position:** The massive cash pile relative to liabilities makes DAWN a lower-risk "burn" candidate compared to peers with less than 12 months of runway. It is well-positioned to weather market volatility.
*   **Institutional Backing:** With approximately **85% institutional ownership**, the stock has strong professional support, which often reduces the likelihood of erratic retail-driven sell-offs.
*   **Beta and Volatility:** A negative Beta (-1.748) suggests the stock often moves inversely to the broader market. This could make DAWN a potential hedge or an "uncorrelated" asset during market downturns.
