### Technical Analysis
The technical shift is equally concerning. The stock’s recent high-volume break below the 50-day moving average ($86.81) serves as a clear signal of institutional distribution. When large funds exit a position on high volume, it often precedes a longer-term correction as the stock seeks a new equilibrium point. The target of the 200-day moving average ($71.47) represents a logical area of support, but the journey to that level implies a significant percentage drop from current prices.

Prioritizing capital preservation and exiting before further multiple compression occurs is the most prudent path.
