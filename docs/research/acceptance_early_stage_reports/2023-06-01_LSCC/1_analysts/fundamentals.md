### **Lattice Semiconductor (LSCC) Fundamental Analysis Report**
**Date of Analysis:** June 1, 2023
**Ticker:** LSCC

---

#### **1. Executive Summary**
Lattice Semiconductor Corporation (LSCC) is a leader in the low-power programmable logic device (FPGA) market. As of June 2023, the company exhibits strong financial momentum characterized by robust revenue growth, industry-leading gross margins (~70%), and a strategic focus on high-growth segments like Industrial, Automotive, and Communications. Despite a high trailing valuation, its forward-looking metrics and PEG ratio suggest that the company is effectively translating market demand into profitable growth.

#### **2. Financial Performance & Statement Analysis**

**Income Statement Trends:**
*   **Revenue Growth:** LSCC has demonstrated consistent top-line growth. Annual revenue increased from $515M in 2021 to $660M in 2022. The first quarter of 2023 (ending March 31, 2023) reported $184.3M in revenue, up significantly from Q1 2022 ($150.5M), representing ~22% YoY growth.
*   **Profitability:** Gross margins are a standout feature, reaching approximately 70% in Q1 2023. Net income for 2022 was $178.9M, a significant jump from $95.9M in 2021.
*   **Operating Efficiency:** The company maintains a lean operating structure with a TTM Operating Margin of 1.83% (according to fundamental data), though quarterly reports suggest operating income is much higher ($59.1M in Q1 2023), indicating that TTM figures may be skewed by one-time items or accounting adjustments.

**Balance Sheet Strength (As of Dec 2022):**
*   **Liquidity:** The company holds $145.7M in cash and equivalents.
*   **Debt Profile:** Total long-term debt stands at approximately $128.7M. With an annual EBITDA of over $226M (2022), LSCC’s debt-to-EBITDA ratio is healthy (below 0.6x), providing significant financial flexibility.
*   **Asset Quality:** Total assets grew to $798.7M in 2022 from $726.5M in 2021, primarily driven by increases in inventory ($110M) and property/equipment.

**Cash Flow Dynamics:**
*   **Operating Cash Flow (OCF):** Strong OCF of $238.8M in 2022, which easily covers capital expenditures ($23.3M), resulting in high Free Cash Flow (FCF).
*   **Shareholder Returns:** LSCC is aggressive in its share repurchase program, spending $110.1M on equity repurchases in 2022, signaling management's confidence in the intrinsic value of the stock.

#### **3. Market & Valuation Metrics**
*   **Market Cap:** $16.26 Billion.
*   **Valuation Ratios:** The Trailing P/E appears abnormally high (5941x) due to specific TTM calculations, but the **Forward P/E of 78.12** and **PEG Ratio of 0.591** provide a clearer picture. A PEG below 1.0 generally suggests the stock may be undervalued relative to its earnings growth potential.
*   **Price Action:** Trading significantly above its 200-day moving average ($75.98) and near its 50-day average ($96.93), indicating a strong upward trend over the past year (52-week low of $43.55).

#### **4. Actionable Insights for Traders**
*   **Growth Play:** Traders should view LSCC as a growth-oriented semiconductor play. The shift toward low-power FPGAs in AI, edge computing, and automotive sectors serves as a long-term tailwind.
*   **Valuation Strategy:** While the Forward P/E of 78x is high for the tech sector, the low PEG ratio (0.59) suggests that the current price is justified if Lattice continues to beat earnings expectations.
*   **Monitoring Capex and R&D:** The company is investing heavily in R&D ($135.7M in 2022). Continued innovation in their "Avant" and "Nexus" platforms is critical for maintaining their 70% gross margin.
*   **Institutional Backing:** With institutional ownership over 100% (likely due to high-conviction holdings and reporting overlaps), the stock has strong professional support, which often reduces the likelihood of "panic selling" during market volatility.
