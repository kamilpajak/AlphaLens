This fundamental analysis report provides a comprehensive overview of Lattice Semiconductor Corporation (LSCC) as of May 18, 2023.

### 1. Executive Summary
Lattice Semiconductor (LSCC) is a high-growth leader in the low-power, small-form-factor Field Programmable Gate Array (FPGA) market. The company has demonstrated exceptional financial performance over the last three years, characterized by strong revenue growth and significant margin expansion. While current valuation multiples are high, the company’s strong competitive moat in low-power applications and its disciplined capital allocation (debt reduction and share buybacks) make it a compelling growth story in the semiconductor sector.

### 2. Company Profile
*   **Sector:** Technology
*   **Industry:** Semiconductors
*   **Focus:** Specialized programmable logic devices (FPGAs) and related software.
*   **Key Markets:** Industrial & Automotive (largest growth drivers), Communications & Computing, and Consumer.
*   **Strategy:** Lattice focuses on the niche for small, low-power FPGAs where larger competitors (like Xilinx/AMD and Intel) often do not prioritize, allowing it to maintain high margins and a strong market position in "edge" computing.

### 3. Financial Statement Analysis

#### Income Statement Trends
*   **Revenue Growth:** Revenue has seen a steady upward trajectory, growing from **$408.1M in 2020** to **$660.4M in 2022**. This represents a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of approximately **27%**.
*   **Profitability:** Net income has grown even more aggressively, from **$47.4M in 2020** to **$178.9M in 2022**, a nearly **277% increase** over two years. This highlights significant operating leverage as the company scales.
*   **Recent Performance:** The most recent quarter (Q1 2023, ending March 31) showed revenue of **$184.3M** and net income of **$55.9M**, indicating that the growth momentum is continuing into the 2023 fiscal year.

#### Balance Sheet Strength
*   **Liquidity:** As of Q1 2023, Lattice held **$112.1M in cash** with a total current asset base of **$348.9M**. Its current ratio is a robust **3.5x**, suggesting excellent short-term liquidity.
*   **Debt Management:** The company has been aggressively paying down long-term debt. Long-term debt decreased from **$140.8M in 2021** to **$103.8M by March 2023**.
*   **Equity:** Total shareholder equity grew to **$515.0M** by March 2023, up from **$411.6M** at the end of 2021, reflecting the company’s transition into sustained high profitability.

#### Cash Flow Analysis
*   **Cash Generation:** Lattice is a "cash-cow" in its niche. Operating cash flow for 2022 was **$238.8M**, significantly exceeding its net income of $178.9M.
*   **Free Cash Flow (FCF):** 2022 FCF was approximately **$215.5M**.
*   **Capital Allocation:** The company uses its FCF for two primary shareholder-friendly activities:
    1.  **Debt Repayment:** Systematically reducing its leverage.
    2.  **Share Repurchases:** In 2022, the company repurchased approximately **$110.1M** of its own stock.

### 5. Actionable Insights for Traders
*   **Growth Momentum:** LSCC is outperforming the broader semiconductor index (SOXX) through its focus on Industrial and Automotive FPGAs, which are less sensitive to consumer electronics cycles.
*   **Efficiency Play:** The widening gap between operating cash flow and net income suggests high-quality earnings with low capital intensity.
*   **Support Levels:** Traders should monitor the **$96-97** level (50-day moving average) as a potential entry point or support.
*   **Risk Factors:** As a high-multiple growth stock, LSCC is sensitive to interest rate changes. Any slowdown in the automotive or industrial sectors could impact its premium valuation.
