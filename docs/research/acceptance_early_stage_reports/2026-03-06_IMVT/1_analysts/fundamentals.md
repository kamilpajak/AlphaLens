#### **Executive Summary**
Immunovant, Inc. (IMVT) is a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company focused on developing novel, targeted therapies for patients with autoimmune diseases. The company is currently in a high-expenditure phase typical of clinical-stage biotech firms, with zero revenue but a significantly strengthened balance sheet following recent capital raises. With a market capitalization of approximately **$5.81 billion** and a cash runway extending over two years, Immunovant is well-positioned to fund its advanced clinical programs.

---

#### **Financial Performance & Statement Analysis**

**1. Income Statement Highlights:**
*   **Revenue:** Consistent with its clinical stage, Immunovant reported **$0 revenue** for the trailing twelve months (TTM).
*   **Net Loss:** The net loss has widened as clinical activities scale. For the quarter ending December 31, 2025, the net loss was **$110.6 million**, compared to **$111.1 million** in the same period the previous year.
*   **Operating Expenses:** R&D remains the primary driver of expenditures, totaling **$98.9 million** for the latest quarter. This represents a significant investment in its monoclonal antibody pipeline. SG&A expenses were relatively stable at **$15.4 million**.
*   **Other Income:** The company leverages its large cash balance to generate interest income, which contributed **$5.3 million** in the most recent quarter, partially offsetting operating losses.

**2. Balance Sheet & Liquidity:**
*   **Cash Position:** As of December 31, 2025, Immunovant held **$994.5 million** in cash and cash equivalents. This is a substantial increase from **$521.9 million** in the previous quarter, indicating a successful capital raise.
*   **Debt Profile:** The company maintains an exceptionally clean balance sheet with **no long-term debt** and total liabilities of only **$66.3 million**.
*   **Shareholder Equity:** Total shareholder equity stands at **$986.1 million**, bolstered by recent financing activities.

**3. Cash Flow Insights:**
*   **Burn Rate:** The quarterly operating cash burn is approximately **$100 million**.
*   **Cash Runway:** Based on the current cash reserves of ~$994.5 million and a burn rate of ~$100 million per quarter, Immunovant has a **runway of approximately 10 quarters (2.5 years)**. This provides a significant cushion to reach major clinical milestones without the immediate need for dilutive financing.
*   **Financing:** The cash flow statement reveals a massive **$564.6 million** inflow from financing activities in the quarter ending December 31, 2025, confirming a large-scale equity issuance.
