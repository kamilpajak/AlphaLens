### **Innoviz Technologies Ltd. (INVZ) Fundamental Analysis Report**
**Date:** November 25, 2024  
**Ticker:** INVZ  
**Sector:** Consumer Cyclical / Auto Parts  

---

#### **1. Company Profile**
Innoviz Technologies Ltd., headquartered in Rosh HaAyin, Israel, is a leading provider of high-performance, solid-state LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) sensors and perception software. Their technology is a critical component for autonomous vehicles (AVs), providing 3D sensing capabilities that enable machines to "see" their surroundings. The company targets mass-market adoption through partnerships with major Tier 1 automotive suppliers and Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) like BMW and Volkswagen.

#### **2. Financial Performance & Earnings Trend**
Innoviz is in a high-growth but pre-profitability stage, characteristic of the LiDAR industry.
*   **Revenue Growth:** The company has shown significant top-line expansion. Revenue for the trailing twelve months (TTM) stands at **$55.09M**. Recent quarterly data indicates a volatile but upward trend, with a peak revenue of approximately **$17.39M** in the most recent quarterly cycle (labeled Q1 2025 in data, likely 2024). 
*   **Profitability:** The company remains unprofitable. Net losses range between **$12M and $21M per quarter**. 
    *   **Gross Profit (TTM):** $12.91M.
    *   **Operating Margin (TTM):** -165.1%, reflecting high R&D and SG&A expenses required to scale.
    *   **EBITDA (TTM):** -$62.38M.

#### **3. Balance Sheet & Liquidity Analysis**
The balance sheet shows a company managing its "cash runway" carefully as it moves toward mass production.
*   **Cash Position:** As of the latest report, Innoviz holds **$8.65M** in cash and **$63.48M** in short-term investments, totaling roughly **$72.13M** in liquid assets.
*   **Debt:** Total debt stands at **$64.55M**, largely composed of lease obligations and short-term debt. 
*   **Shareholder Equity:** Total equity is **$77.72M**. The **Price-to-Book ratio is 1.82**, suggesting the market values the company at nearly twice its accounting net worth, which is common for tech-heavy firms with significant intellectual property.
*   **Burn Rate:** With a quarterly net loss average of ~$17M and ~$72M in liquid assets, the company has a runway of approximately **4 quarters** (1 year) unless they reduce expenses, increase revenue significantly, or raise further capital.

#### **4. Market Valuation & Analyst Sentiment**
*   **Market Cap:** $141.48M.
*   **Valuation Ratios:** The **Price-to-Sales (TTM) ratio is 2.57x**, which is relatively modest for a high-tech growth company, reflecting market skepticism regarding the path to profitability and the timing of AV adoption.
*   **Analyst Outlook:** Sentiment is remarkably positive despite the share price decline. There are **3 "Buy" or "Strong Buy" ratings** and zero "Hold" or "Sell" ratings. The average **Analyst Target Price is $2.03**, representing a significant upside from current trading levels (near the 50-day moving average of $0.78).

#### **5. Actionable Insights for Traders**
*   **Bottom-Fishing Opportunity:** The stock is trading well below its **200-day moving average ($1.36)** and near its **50-day moving average ($0.78)**. With a 52-week low of **$0.58**, the downside may be somewhat limited if the company can maintain its OEM partnerships.
*   **Monitoring the Runway:** Traders should closely watch upcoming earnings for any signs of "at-the-market" (ATM) offerings or debt financing, as the current cash position suggests a need for capital within the next 12 months.
*   **LiDAR Sector Beta:** With a **Beta of 1.52**, INVZ is highly volatile and moves aggressively with the broader tech and EV sectors. It is a high-risk, high-reward play.
*   **Revenue Milestone Focus:** The jump in quarterly revenue from ~$6M to ~$17M in recent periods is the most bullish signal. If this trend continues, the path to break-even becomes clearer, which could trigger a massive re-rating toward the $2.00 analyst target.
