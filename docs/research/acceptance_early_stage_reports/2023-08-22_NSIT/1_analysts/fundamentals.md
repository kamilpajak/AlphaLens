### Fundamental Analysis Report: Insight Enterprises, Inc. (NSIT)
**Date:** August 22, 2023
**Ticker:** NSIT
**Sector:** Technology | **Industry:** Electronics & Computer Distribution

---

#### **1. Company Profile**
Insight Enterprises, Inc. is a global provider of information technology (IT) hardware, software, and services solutions. Headquartered in Chandler, Arizona, the company helps organizations of all sizes manage their IT environments through digital transformation, modern infrastructure, and cybersecurity solutions. Operating across the U.S., Canada, Europe, and Asia-Pacific, NSIT acts as a critical link between technology manufacturers and end-users.

#### **2. Financial Performance & Profitability**
*   **Revenue Trends:** NSIT reported TTM revenue of **$8.25 billion**. While quarterly revenue growth showed a slight dip of **-1.2% YOY**, the company has demonstrated a consistent ability to generate massive volume.
*   **Earnings Growth:** Despite the revenue headwind, NSIT achieved a remarkable **66.5% YOY growth in quarterly earnings**. This discrepancy suggests a significant improvement in operational efficiency or a shift toward higher-margin service contracts rather than just hardware distribution.
*   **Margins:**
    *   **Gross Margin:** Currently around **21.3%** (TTM Gross Profit $1.76B on $8.25B Revenue), showing improvement from historical levels (approx. 15.7% in 2022).
    *   **Operating Margin:** **7.08%**.
    *   **Profit Margin:** **1.91%**.
    *   The low net profit margin is characteristic of the distribution industry, but the expanding gross margin indicates a successful transition toward value-added services.

#### **3. Valuation & Market Metrics**
*   **Price-to-Earnings (P/E):** The trailing P/E is **15.73**, while the **Forward P/E is a very attractive 7.05**.
*   **PEG Ratio:** At **0.783**, the stock is technically undervalued relative to its expected earnings growth (a PEG < 1 is generally considered a "Buy" signal for value investors).
*   **Price-to-Sales (P/S):** **0.287**, reflecting the high-volume/low-margin nature of the business.
*   **Institutional Interest:** Institutional ownership is exceptionally high at **105.1%** (suggesting high conviction and potential short interest hedging).

#### **4. Financial Health & Solvency (Balance Sheet)**
*   **Liquidity:** The company holds **$195.95 million** in cash as of June 2023. With total current assets of **$4.34 billion** and current liabilities of **$3.31 billion**, the current ratio is **1.31**, indicating healthy short-term solvency.
*   **Debt Profile:** Total debt stands at approximately **$1.02 billion** ($680M short-term, $338M long-term). The **Debt-to-Equity ratio of 0.65** is manageable for a company with consistent cash flows.
*   **Asset Structure:** The balance sheet is heavily weighted in Receivables (**$3.67 billion**), which is typical for IT distributors but requires careful credit management.

#### **5. Cash Flow & Shareholder Returns**
*   **Free Cash Flow (FCF):** The company maintains positive operating cash flow, though it fluctuates quarterly due to working capital needs.
*   **Share Buybacks:** NSIT does not pay a dividend but is highly aggressive in returning capital through buybacks. In Q2 2023 alone, the company spent **$99.98 million** on share repurchases, reducing the share count and supporting EPS growth.

---

#### **Actionable Insights for Traders**
1.  **Value Play:** NSIT’s Forward P/E of 7.05 and PEG of 0.78 suggest significant upside potential if the company continues to execute its margin-expansion strategy. It is trading at a discount compared to its growth rate.
2.  **Margin Expansion Catalyst:** The surge in earnings (+66.5%) despite flat revenue indicates that NSIT is successfully pivotting to higher-margin software and cloud services. Traders should watch for further gross margin improvements in upcoming quarters.
3.  **Buyback Support:** The aggressive share repurchase program provides a floor for the stock price and increases the ownership value for remaining shareholders.
4.  **Institutional Stability:** High institutional ownership provides a level of professional stability, though it may limit retail liquidity.
