# Fundamental Analysis Report: ACM Research, Inc. (ACMR)
**Date of Analysis:** 2023-08-22
**Ticker:** ACMR
**Sector:** Technology | **Industry:** Semiconductor Equipment & Materials

---

## 1. Company Profile
ACM Research, Inc. (ACMR) is a leader in the development, manufacturing, and sale of single-wafer wet cleaning equipment. These tools are critical in the semiconductor manufacturing process, specifically designed to improve production yields and the performance of advanced chips. The company’s proprietary technologies, such as **SAPS** (Space Alternated Phase Shift) and **TEBO** (Timely Energized Bubble Oscillation), address the challenges of cleaning delicate features on wafers without causing structural damage. Headquartered in Fremont, California, ACMR operates primarily through its subsidiaries in China, serving major global chipmakers.

## 2. Financial Performance Over Time

### Revenue and Profitability Trends
ACMR has demonstrated exceptional top-line growth over the past several years, driven by the expansion of the semiconductor industry and increasing complexity in chip manufacturing.
*   **Annual Revenue Growth:** Revenue surged from **$107.5M in 2019** to **$388.8M in 2022**, representing a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of approximately **53%**.
*   **Net Income:** Net income grew from **$18.9M in 2019** to **$39.3M in 2022**. While profitability has increased, the growth in net income has occasionally lagged revenue due to heavy investments in R&D and expansion.
*   **Margins:** As of the trailing twelve months (TTM), the company maintains a **Gross Profit Margin of ~44.4%** and a **Profit Margin of 10.4%**. The operating margin stands at **9.42%**, reflecting significant operating leverage as the company scales.

### Recent Quarterly Performance (2023)
The data for 2023 indicates a strong acceleration in performance:
*   **Q1 2023 (Ended March 31):** Revenue of $74.3M with Net Income of $7.1M.
*   **Q2 2023 (Ended June 30):** Revenue jumped significantly to **$144.6M**, a nearly 100% increase quarter-over-quarter, with Net Income reaching **$26.8M**. This sudden surge underscores the company's ability to fulfill large orders and suggests strong demand for its new product lines.

## 3. Balance Sheet and Financial Health
ACMR maintains a robust balance sheet capable of supporting its aggressive growth strategy.
*   **Liquidity:** As of June 30, 2023, the company held **$257.4M in Cash and Cash Equivalents**.
*   **Inventory Management:** Inventory levels have increased to **$471.1M**. In the semiconductor equipment industry, high inventory often signals a strong backlog of orders and work-in-progress machines destined for major customers.
*   **Solvency:** Total Liabilities stand at **$483.5M** against Total Assets of **$1.32B**. Total Shareholder Equity has reached **$691.9M**, showing consistent growth from $141M in 2020.
*   **Debt:** The company has manageable debt levels, with approximately **$80.9M in total debt** (short and long-term), well-covered by its cash position.

## 4. Cash Flow Analysis
*   **Operating Cash Flow:** Often volatile due to high working capital requirements (inventory building). Q2 2023 saw a positive operating cash flow of **$11.3M**, a recovery from the $30M outflow in Q1 2023.
*   **Capital Expenditures:** ACMR consistently reinvests in its facilities, with CapEx of **$15M in Q1 2023** and **$7.8M in Q2 2023**.
*   **Financing:** The company has utilized equity and debt markets strategically to fund its rapid expansion in China and its newer production facilities.

## 5. Market Valuation and Analyst Sentiment

## 6. Actionable Insights for Traders
*   **Growth Play:** ACMR is a high-beta (1.68) growth stock. Traders should look for entry points during sector-wide pullbacks, as its fundamental growth outpaces many peers.
*   **Inventory as a Catalyst:** The massive jump in inventory ($393M at end of 2022 to $471M in mid-2023) combined with the revenue spike in Q2 suggests that ACMR is entering a period of high-volume deliveries.
*   **Market Position:** As chipmakers move to 5nm and 3nm processes, the requirement for sophisticated wet cleaning (where ACMR excels) increases. Its specialized technology provides a competitive moat.
*   **Risk Note:** Heavy reliance on the Chinese market and geopolitical tensions in the semiconductor space remain the primary risks. Traders should monitor U.S.-China trade relations closely.
