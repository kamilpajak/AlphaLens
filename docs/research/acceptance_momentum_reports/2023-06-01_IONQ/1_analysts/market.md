The following technical analysis for **IONQ** as of **June 1, 2023**, evaluates the stock's recent parabolic move and identifies key levels for traders.

### **Market Context & Trend Analysis**
IONQ has undergone a massive bullish transformation over the last 30 days. After languishing near its 200-day SMA in early May, the stock exploded from approximately **$5.30 to a peak of nearly $11.00**, representing a ~100% gain in a single month. This move has been characterized by high volume and increasing volatility.

### **Technical Indicator Analysis**

#### **1. Trend & Moving Averages (Bullish)**
*   **50-Day & 200-Day SMA:** The stock is trading significantly above both its **50 SMA ($6.94)** and **200 SMA ($5.47)**. The wide gap between the current price ($10.79) and these averages indicates a highly extended "overbought" state in the medium-to-long term. However, the upward slope of the 50 SMA is steep, confirming a powerful momentum-driven trend.
*   **Golden Cross:** The 50 SMA is well above the 200 SMA, providing a long-term bullish signal that was triggered earlier in the year and is now in a "runaway" phase.

#### **2. Momentum Indicators (Slightly Overheated)**
*   **RSI (68.75):** The RSI reached extreme levels above **81** on May 23rd, indicating the stock was severely overbought. It has since cooled to **68.75** as the price consolidated between $9.50 and $11.00. While no longer at "nosebleed" levels, an RSI near 70 suggests the bullish trend remains intact but the easy "early" money has been made.
*   **MACD (1.23):** The MACD remains positive and high. The histogram showed a peak in momentum in late May and has since leveled off, suggesting that while the price is still rising, the *rate* of acceleration is beginning to slow.

#### **3. Volatility & Volume (High Risk/High Reward)**
*   **Bollinger Bands:** The price is trading near the **Upper Band ($12.36)**, but the bands themselves have widened dramatically (Lower Band at **$4.67**). This "opening of the jaws" indicates a massive increase in volatility. 
*   **ATR (0.88):** The Average True Range has more than doubled from early May (0.36). This means daily swings of 8-10% are now the norm for IONQ. Traders must adjust position sizes downward to account for this increased risk.
*   **VWMA ($9.61):** The Volume Weighted Moving Average is currently at $9.61. Since the price ($10.79) is above the VWMA, it indicates that the average buyer over the last period is in profit, providing a "cushion" of support if the stock pulls back.

### **Actionable Insights**
*   **Support Levels:** Immediate support is found at the **VWMA ($9.61)**. A deeper correction could see the stock test the psychological **$9.00** level or the **50 SMA ($6.94)**, though a drop to the latter would represent a significant trend breakdown.
*   **Resistance Levels:** The current "ceiling" is the **Upper Bollinger Band ($12.36)** and the recent local high near **$11.65**.
*   **Risk Management:** Given the ATR of 0.88, a technical stop-loss should be placed no closer than $1.75 below entry (2x ATR) to avoid being "stopped out" by normal market noise.
