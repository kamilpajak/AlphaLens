### **Executive Summary**
**ONTO** is currently experiencing a powerful bullish breakout. After consolidating in the $80–$88 range during late April and early May, the stock has entered a vertical ascent, gaining nearly 20% in the last 10 trading sessions. While all trend indicators confirm a strong bull market, momentum oscillators indicate the stock is entering an "overbought" phase, suggesting that while the trend is strong, the entry risk for new positions is currently elevated.

---

### **1. Trend Analysis: Strong Multi-Timeframe Alignment**
The price action shows a textbook bullish alignment across short, medium, and long-term moving averages.
*   **Long-Term (200 SMA):** The stock is trading significantly above its 200-day SMA ($77.21). The price has maintained a healthy distance from this level since late 2022, confirming a structural uptrend.
*   **Medium-Term (50 SMA):** The 50-day SMA stands at $84.84. The recent breakout above this level on May 5th served as the catalyst for the current leg up.
*   **Short-Term (10 EMA):** The 10-day EMA ($92.05) is acting as immediate dynamic support. The price is currently extended above this EMA, which often precedes a "reversion to the mean" or a sideways consolidation to let the average catch up.

### **2. Momentum and Volatility**
*   **MACD (Momentum):** The MACD is at 3.64 and rising sharply. The gap between the MACD line and the signal line is widening, which indicates that the buying pressure is still accelerating. There are no signs of bearish divergence yet.
*   **RSI (Overbought/Oversold):** The RSI has reached **78.46**. Typically, a reading above 70 indicates an overbought condition. While RSI can remain high during strong trends, this level suggests that the "easy money" of the current move has been made, and a period of profit-taking is likely.
*   **Bollinger Bands:** The price ($97.95) is currently "walking" the Upper Bollinger Band ($98.67). The bands are expanding rapidly, which signifies a surge in volatility. Trading outside or near the upper band often indicates a temporary price extreme.

### **3. Support and Resistance Levels**
*   **Immediate Resistance:** **$100.00**. This is a major psychological milestone. Given the current proximity ($97.95) and overbought RSI, expect significant selling pressure or a "stall" at this level.
*   **Primary Support:** **$92.00 – $93.00**. This aligns with the 10-day EMA and the previous highs from May 15.
*   **Secondary Support:** **$85.00 – $86.00**. This zone contains the 50-day SMA and the Bollinger Middle Band, representing a "value" area if a deeper correction occurs.
