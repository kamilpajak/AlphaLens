## 1. Market Context & Trend Analysis
LITE (Lumentum Holdings Inc.) has recently emerged from a significant period of downward pressure that saw the stock decline from its January highs of ~$107 to a recent low near $81 in late April. However, the price action on **May 4, 2022**, marked a potential structural shift. *   **Short-Term Trend (Bullish):** The stock is trading well above its **10-day EMA ($85.50)** and **VWMA ($85.56)**. This indicates that the recent buyers are "in the money" and short-term momentum is firmly behind the bulls.

This suggests there is still room for further upside before the stock becomes overbought. The **MACD (-1.86)** remains negative but is rising sharply toward its signal line, showing bullish convergence.
*   **Volatility (Bollinger Bands & ATR):** The price is currently testing the **Upper Bollinger Band ($91.63)**. Historically, a "tag" of the upper band following a high-volume spike can lead to either a breakout or a short-term pause. The **ATR of 3.38** indicates that daily swings are significant; traders should account for this volatility when setting stop-losses.

## 3. Volume Confirmation
The **VWMA ($85.56)** serves as a critical "line in the sand." Because the current price (~$88.82) is above the VWMA on high volume, it confirms that the recent price move is supported by significant capital commitment, rather than just low-volume speculation.

## 4. Actionable Insights & Strategic Recommendations
