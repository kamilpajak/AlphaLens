### **Trend Analysis**
**ACMR** is currently in a confirmed long-term and medium-term bullish trend.
- **Moving Averages:** The stock is trading significantly above its **200-day SMA ($11.52)** and **50-day SMA ($14.32)**. A "Golden Cross" (50 SMA crossing above 200 SMA) occurred earlier this year, and the widening gap between these averages indicates strengthening momentum.

- **Volume Confirmation:** The **VWMA ($17.03)** is below the current price but trending upward. The fact that the price is significantly above the volume-weighted average indicates that the recent rally was supported by strong buying conviction.

- **RSI (Relative Strength Index):** The RSI reached an extreme overbought level of **78.78 on September 5**, coinciding with a local price peak of $19.63. As of September 11, the RSI has "cooled off" to **68.49**. This is a healthy development, suggesting the stock is digesting its gains without a sharp reversal.
- **MACD:** The MACD remains deep in positive territory (**1.51**), though the histogram shows a slight flattening of momentum. This suggests that while the bulls are in control, the "explosive" phase of the move may be transitioning into a consolidation phase.
- **Bollinger Bands:** The stock is trading in the upper quadrant of the Bollinger Bands. With the **Upper Band at $20.70**, there is still room for an upside move before hitting significant technical resistance, while the middle band (~$16.00) would be the target for a deeper correction.

### **Actionable Insights**
1.  **Support Levels:** The primary support zone is between **$17.90 (10 EMA)** and **$17.00 (VWMA)**. A successful test of these levels would provide a low-risk entry point for trend-followers.
2.  **Resistance Levels:** Immediate psychological resistance sits at **$20.00**, followed by the Bollinger Upper Band at **$20.70**.
3.  **Risk Management:** Investors should watch the RSI. If it begins to form a lower high while the price makes a higher high (bearish divergence), it may signal an exhausted trend. A close below the 10 EMA ($17.92) on high volume would be a signal to tighten stop-losses or take partial profits.
