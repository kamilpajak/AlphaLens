## Fundamental Analysis Report: Insight Enterprises, Inc. (NSIT)
**Date:** December 21, 2023
**Ticker:** NSIT
**Sector:** Technology
**Industry:** Electronics & Computer Distribution

---

### **1. Executive Summary**
Insight Enterprises, Inc. (NSIT) is a global provider of information technology (IT) hardware, software, and service solutions. Over the past three years, the company has navigated a complex macro environment characterized by high hardware demand during the pandemic followed by a recent cooling. However, NSIT is actively transitioning its business model toward higher-margin software and cloud services. Current valuation metrics, particularly a Forward P/E of 7.05 and a PEG ratio of 0.78, suggest the stock may be undervalued relative to its growth potential and historical performance.

---

### **2. Financial Document Analysis**

#### **Income Statement Trends**
*   **Revenue Performance:** NSIT reached a peak revenue of approximately **$10.43 billion** in 2022. However, the Trailing Twelve Month (TTM) revenue as of late 2023 has declined to **$8.25 billion**, a 20% drop. This reflects a normalization in hardware sales after the pandemic-induced surge.
*   **Net Income & Margins:** Net income followed a similar trend, dropping from $280.6 million in 2022 to a TTM figure of **$157.3 million**. Despite the revenue decline, the company maintains an operating margin of **7.08%** and a gross profit of **$1.76 billion** TTM, suggesting that the company is successfully defending its margins by focusing on higher-value services.
*   **Earnings per Share (EPS):** Diluted EPS (TTM) stands at **$4.86**.

#### **Balance Sheet Health**
*   **Liquidity:** The company holds approximately **$163.6 million** in cash (as of the end of 2022). Current assets ($3.9B) significantly outweigh current liabilities ($2.87B), providing a healthy current ratio.
*   **Debt Profile:** Total debt was reported at **$939 million** at the end of 2022, with a Net Debt position of **$775.6 million**. Given an EBITDA of **$552.8 million**, the Debt/EBITDA ratio is roughly **1.7x**, which is conservative for the industry and indicates manageable leverage.
*   **Shareholder Equity:** Book value per share is **$53.20**, providing a solid floor for the valuation.

#### **Cash Flow Dynamics**
*   **Operational Cash Flow:** Cash flow from operations has been volatile, hitting **$355 million** in 2020 before dipping to **$98 million** in 2022. This volatility is common in distribution businesses due to working capital swings (inventory and receivables).
*   **Share Repurchases:** NSIT has been aggressive in returning capital to shareholders, spending over **$107.9 million** on share repurchases in 2022 alone. This demonstrates management's confidence that the stock is undervalued.

---

### **3. Valuation and Market Sentiment**
*   **P/E Ratio:** The trailing P/E of **15.73** is moderate, but the **Forward P/E of 7.05** is exceptionally low. This disparity suggests the market expects a significant earnings recovery or is currently mispricing the stock's future earnings power.
*   **PEG Ratio:** At **0.783**, the PEG ratio indicates that NSIT is "cheap" relative to its earnings growth rate (a PEG < 1 is typically considered undervalued).
*   **Analyst Outlook:** The average analyst target price is **$101.25**. With the stock trading around its 200-day moving average of **$99.83** and recently bouncing off a 50-day average of **$78.23**, the stock appears to be in a consolidation or recovery phase.

---

### **4. Actionable Insights for Traders**

*   **Valuation Arbitrage Opportunity:** The Forward P/E of 7.05 is roughly half the trailing P/E. Traders should look for confirmation of earnings growth in the next quarterly report. If EPS meets or exceeds expectations, a re-rating toward a mid-teen multiple could drive significant price appreciation.
*   **Strategic Pivot Support:** Monitor the "Services" and "Cloud" revenue segments. As NSIT shifts away from low-margin hardware distribution toward high-margin IT consulting and software solutions, gross margins should expand. Supporting evidence is found in the TTM Gross Profit holding steady ($1.76B) despite the drop in Total Revenue.
*   **Technical Support Levels:** The 200-day moving average near $100 acts as a critical psychological and technical level. A sustained break above this level with volume could signal a move back toward the 52-week high of $148.58.
*   **Institutional Backing:** Institutional ownership is recorded at over **100%** (likely including short interest and reporting lags), indicating high conviction from professional money managers.
