### **MACOM Technology Solutions Holdings, Inc. (MTSI) Fundamental Analysis Report**

**Date:** August 8, 2023  
**Ticker:** MTSI

#### **1. Executive Summary**
MACOM Technology Solutions Holdings, Inc. (MTSI) is a leading provider of high-performance analog semiconductor solutions for use in wireless and wired applications. As of August 2023, the company exhibits strong growth characteristics and a solid balance sheet, but its premium valuation suggests that the market has priced in significant future performance. Analysts are overwhelmingly bullish, targeting a price of approximately $273.07.

#### **2. Company Profile**
MACOM designs and manufactures semiconductors for the radio frequency (RF), microwave, millimeter-wave, and light-wave spectrums. Their products are critical components in 5G network infrastructure, data centers, satellite communications, and aerospace/defense systems. This positioning allows them to benefit from long-term secular trends in data consumption and telecommunications upgrades.

#### **3. Financial Performance Analysis**
*   **Revenue Growth:** MTSI has shown resilient growth, with TTM revenue reaching $1.02 billion. Recent quarterly data indicates a year-over-year revenue growth of 24.5%, driven by demand in the data center and industrial/defense sectors.
*   **Profitability:** The company maintains a healthy profit margin of 15.9%. Net income for the fiscal year 2023 was approximately $91.6 million. Earnings growth remains robust, with a 54.4% YOY increase in the most recent quarter.
*   **Cash Flow:** MACOM generates strong operating cash flows ($166.9 million in FY 2023). This cash generation supports its R&D efforts (which amounted to $148.5 million in 2023) and occasional strategic acquisitions.

#### **4. Valuation and Market Sentiment**
*   **Premium Multiples:** MTSI is currently trading at a Trailing P/E of 126.61 and a Forward P/E of 63.69. These levels are high even for the semiconductor industry, reflecting high investor confidence in the company's growth story.
*   **Market Position:** With a Market Capitalization of ~$21.1 billion and a Price-to-Sales ratio of 20.66, the stock is positioned as a high-growth "glamour" stock.
*   **Analyst Outlook:** The sentiment is strongly positive, with 12 analysts rating it as a "Buy" or "Strong Buy" and only 3 as a "Hold." No sell ratings exist, indicating a consensus expectation for continued outperformance.

#### **5. Balance Sheet and Solvency**
*   **Liquidity:** The company is in a strong liquidity position, with roughly $514 million in cash and short-term investments against total current liabilities of $83.5 million (as of Sept 2023 report).
*   **Debt Profile:** Long-term debt stands at $447 million. Given the company's EBITDA generation and cash reserves, the debt-to-equity ratio is manageable and does not present an immediate solvency risk.

#### **6. Actionable Insights for Traders**
*   **Growth Play:** MTSI is an ideal candidate for growth-oriented portfolios looking for exposure to 5G and data center infrastructure.
*   **Technical Entry:** With a 50-day moving average of $238.36 and a 200-day average of $176.21, the stock has been in a strong uptrend. Traders should look for pullbacks toward the 50-day MA for potential entry points.
*   **Valuation Warning:** The high EV/EBITDA (79.29) makes the stock sensitive to interest rate changes and earnings misses. Traders should monitor quarterly earnings calls closely for any guidance adjustments.
