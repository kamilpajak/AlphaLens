### Relay Therapeutics, Inc. (RLAY) Fundamental Analysis Report

**Current Date:** March 6, 2026
**Ticker:** RLAY
**Sector:** Healthcare (Biotechnology)

---

#### **1. Company Profile**
Relay Therapeutics is a clinical-stage precision medicine company focused on transforming drug discovery by combining computational and experimental technologies. The company’s proprietary "Dynamo" platform is designed to observe protein motion in real-time, allowing for the development of targeted therapies for complex diseases, particularly in oncology and genetic disorders. Based in Cambridge, Massachusetts, Relay relies heavily on its technological edge to identify novel binding sites that were previously considered "undruggable."

#### **2. Financial Performance (Income Statement Analysis)**
Relay Therapeutics exhibits the classic financial profile of a high-growth, clinical-stage biotech firm: minimal revenue and substantial net losses driven by intensive Research & Development (R&D) spending.

*   **Revenue Trends:** Revenue is primarily derived from collaboration agreements and is highly volatile. For the fiscal year ending Dec 31, 2025, RLAY reported **$15.36 million** in revenue, an increase from $10.01 million in 2024, though significantly lower than the $25.55 million recognized in 2023.
*   **Net Losses:** The company reported a net loss of **$276.48 million** in 2025, showing an improvement from the $337.71 million loss in 2024 and $341.97 million in 2023.
*   **Operating Expenses:** R&D remains the largest expense category, totaling **$261.38 million** in 2025. While still high, this is a notable reduction from the $319.09 million spent in 2024, suggesting a strategic tightening of program focus or clinical trial efficiency. SG&A expenses also decreased to $56.71 million from $76.59 million year-over-year.

#### **3. Balance Sheet & Liquidity (Cash Runway)**
As of December 31, 2025, the company’s liquidity position is critical for its survival as a non-profitable biotech.

*   **Cash & Short-Term Investments:** Total liquid assets (Cash + Short-term investments) stood at **$554.52 million** at the end of 2025 ($84.02M cash + $470.5M investments).
*   **Runway Analysis:** With a 2025 operating cash burn of **$235.46 million**, Relay Therapeutics has approximately **2.35 years of runway** remaining (until mid-2028), assuming no major changes in spending or further equity raises.
*   **Debt & Liabilities:** The company maintains a clean balance sheet with **no significant long-term debt**. Total liabilities are $54.27 million, largely comprised of capital lease obligations ($32.46 million) and current payables.
*   **Shareholder Equity:** Total equity remains healthy at $567.06 million, though it has declined from $777.79 million in 2024 due to ongoing net losses.

#### **4. Cash Flow Dynamics**
*   **Operating Cash Flow:** Net cash used in operating activities was -$235.46 million in 2025. The company relies on its investment portfolio to fund operations, as evidenced by $192.8 million in proceeds from the sale/maturation of investments during the year.
*   **Financing:** RLAY has not had a major dilutive equity event in 2025, with financing cash flows showing a modest $1.6 million. This follows a more active 2024 where it raised approximately $270 million through financing activities.

#### **5. Market Valuation & Sentiment**
*   **Market Cap:** ~$2.93 Billion.
*   **Price Performance:** With a current estimated price of **$16.40**, RLAY is trading near its 52-week high ($17.32) and significantly above its 50-day moving average ($10.89) and 200-day moving average ($7.14). This indicates very strong recent momentum.
*   **Institutional Ownership:** At **97.79%**, institutional backing is exceptionally high, suggesting that professional investors have deep confidence in the Dynamo platform and clinical pipeline.
*   **Analyst Outlook:** The sentiment is overwhelmingly bullish. Out of 12 analysts, 10 rate it a "Buy" and 2 rate it a "Strong Buy." The average target price of **$18.00** suggests a further ~10% upside from current levels.

---

### **Actionable Insights for Traders**

*   **Momentum Strategy:** RLAY is in a strong uptrend, trading well above its 50-day and 200-day moving averages. Traders should look for entries on minor pullbacks toward the 50-day MA ($10.89) if the positive sentiment persists.
*   **Risk Profile:** As a clinical-stage biotech, the stock is highly sensitive to clinical trial data readouts. Any failure in its lead oncology programs would likely result in a significant "gap down" in price.
*   **Cash Safety:** The current cash runway of ~2.3 years is relatively comfortable for a biotech of this size, reducing the immediate risk of a forced "dilutive" secondary offering at unfavorable prices. However, a capital raise might be expected by late 2026 or early 2027 to extend the runway through later-stage trials.
*   **Valuation vs. Cash:** The company’s Enterprise Value (EV) is roughly $2.37B ($2.93B Market Cap - $554M Cash). For a platform technology company with high institutional ownership, this valuation is reasonable if the Dynamo platform continues to yield promising candidates.
