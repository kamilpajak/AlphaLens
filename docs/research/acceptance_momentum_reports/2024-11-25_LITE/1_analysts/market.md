## Lumentum Holdings Inc. (LITE) Technical Analysis Report

**Current Date:** 2024-11-25

### Executive Summary
Lumentum Holdings Inc. (`LITE`) is currently exhibiting a **strong bullish momentum**, characterized by a significant price breakout in early November. ### Indicator Analysis

#### 1. Trend Analysis: Moving Averages (50 SMA, 200 SMA)
*   **Close 50-day SMA:** $70.04
*   **Close 200-day SMA:** $54.06
*   **Observation:** `LITE` is trading significantly above both its 50-day and 200-day Simple Moving Averages. The price is ~27% above the 50 SMA and ~65% above the 200 SMA. This confirms a robust "Golden" market structure where the short-term trend is accelerating faster than the long-term trend. 
*   **Insight:** The wide gap between the current price and the 200 SMA suggests the stock is in a "parabolic" phase. While bullish, this increases the risk of a mean-reversion event if the trend exhausts.

#### 2. Momentum Analysis: MACD & RSI
*   **MACD (5.41) vs. Signal (4.94):** The MACD remains above the signal line, indicating that positive momentum is still dominant. However, the gap is relatively narrow, suggesting that the rate of acceleration is stabilizing.
*   **RSI (68.16):** The RSI peaked at a very high **81.85** on November 13, indicating extremely overbought conditions. The subsequent dip to 68.16 is a healthy sign; it shows the "froth" is leaving the market through time (consolidation) rather than a sharp price drop. It remains just below the 70 threshold, leaving room for another leg up.

#### 3. Volatility and Price Extremes: Bollinger Bands
*   **Bollinger Upper Band (UB):** $96.78
*   **Bollinger Lower Band (LB):** $58.77
*   **Observation:** The bands have widened dramatically, reflecting the high volatility of the November move. The current price of $89.09 is comfortably within the bands, though it was "riding" the upper band during the initial surge.
*   **Insight:** The Upper Band at $96.78 serves as the immediate psychological and technical resistance target for the current move.

#### 4. Volume-Weighted Analysis: VWMA
*   **VWMA:** $83.07
*   **Observation:** The Volume Weighted Moving Average (VWMA) is currently at $83.07. 
*   **Insight:** Since the price is holding above the VWMA, we can conclude that the average buyer during this recent high-volume period is "in the money." The $83 level represents a critical "Value" zone. If the price retests this level, it is likely to act as strong support.
